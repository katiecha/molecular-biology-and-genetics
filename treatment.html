Treatment

Overview

The treatment of tumors, both benign and malignant, requires a team of experienced medical professionals. I decided to consult with two oncologists working at the Levine Cancer Institute of Atrium Health to get a different perspective on treatment. To hear from these specialists, check out the following podcast interviews!

 

Interview with Dr. Sarma

Dr. Sarma is a breast surgical oncologist at Atrium Health in Charlotte, NC. She has been working within the Levine Cancer Institute for 7 years, and she completed her breast surgical oncology training at Duke University.

This interview will go over her background, the diagnosis and treatment of benign/malignant tumors, and recent changes in surgical oncology.
